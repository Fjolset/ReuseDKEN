Events

Only the imagination is the limit during our events. Here you will learn about recycling and upcycling, giving you the opportunity to ressurect our available materials. You learn something new every time and there is always the opportunity to get help and to form friendships with the volunteers and other participants at Reuse. We plan around one event a month and there are room for everyone.

Do you have an idea for an event? Then contact us!
