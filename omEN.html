What is Reuse?

Find out about used items sorted in the most common rooms in the home. At Reuse, you can find items and stuff that you can take home - free of charge. Reuse give these things an additional chance to avoid becoming waste.

In fact, things also come from you. Either you have handed them to us or you have given them to us through a recycling station.

Reuse is therefore a place where we share and give to each other. Take only what you actually need. Leave the rest to others.

Loan a bicycle or trolley

If you have found something big on Reuse, you can borrow a bicycle to ship it home. Ask the staff how it works.

Aarhus climate plan

Aarhus Municipality's climate plan combines sustainable urban development with economic growth. The initiative covers projects that develop solutions in energy optimization and climate adaptation, reducing CO2 emissions and making the city a more attractive place to live and work.

Climate efforts are based on partnerships and cooperation between private and public actors, and Aarhus Municipality focuses in particular on engaging companies and citizens at work, as these two groups account for 75% of all CO2 emissions in Aarhus.

Aarhus Municipality is at the forefront of encouraging energy efficiency and alternatives to fossil fuels and plays an active role in cooperation with relevant actors in the work to make the city CO2 neutral by 2030. In addition, since 2012, Aarhus Municipality has entered into more than 40 strategic partnerships with private companies. These partnerships are specifically aimed at linking the climate plan's goals with economic growth.

Project Info

At Reuse we would like to support projects and initiatives that can change the perceptions of waste - and inspire.

Reuse wants to influence the view on waste and show that waste is a resource. We therefore provide space for anyone who can show, prove and convey how it can be done.

Collection

If you have a project at your studio or a small company, we can help you collect materials at REUSE.

Help us support you and your project and let us together show what you can make from recycled materials.

If it sounds like something for you, call us at +45 41 85 79 76 or visit us to talk.

Keep in mind that it may take a long time to collect, so contact us in good time.
