Education

REUSE offers education on recycling and upcycling to school classes.

Aarhusian 4th-7th. classes can get lessons at Reuse and become wiser about waste, resources and recycling. The course increases students' awareness and skills in the fields of waste, resources and recycling.

The course strengthens students' ability to work creatively, innovative and entrepreneurial, and the students progress from the course with increased material knowledge and experience from the outside world.

The teaching is conducted by the Waste Management Aarhus Visiting Service, and complies with the curriculum requirements.
